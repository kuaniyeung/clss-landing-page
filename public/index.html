Who is CLSS?

"Don't worry 'bout it!" - CLSS

This is one of Collin's favourite lines. You might ask, who is CLSS? Why am I reading this? Well, let me tell you all about it.

On one hot summer day in Canada, Collin Laurent "Silly" Stone was born... Just kidding, I'm not about to go from the very beginning. If I do, this website will be too long for me and you to handle. When his bibliography comes out, we'll notify you.

I met Collin at the perfect time where he just made a big move and went from being a chef to a software engineer. This is not where we go deep into his many life stories, but more of a place where we come to appreciate his awesomeness and peculiarity. Without him, this website simply will not exist. If you have a moment, go leave him a message and give him a thumbs-up.

CLSS' Many Talents

Pop Daddy, Talk to Me

From Tupac to the Weeknd, Collin can discuss how pop culture changes the world (literally) with you from sun sets to sun rises (if he chooses to). For anyone who is not familiar with pop culture, it's intriguing to listen to all the stories he has to share. And if you are also a big fan, I would say you should be bffs already.

Impressiveness:
9.5/10
Oh Hey, Wasn't Yesterday Your Birthday?

Other than being exceptionally relevant to pop culture, Collin is also really good with remembering numbers and details. We're not talking about algebra or mental arithemetic, but how he would recall details of a conversation from months ago or cite your birthdate from that one time you mentioned it. It's almost creepy, but nonetheless impressive.

Impressiveness:
10/10
Perfect Eggs for Days

Collin is THE egg master. He acquired this talent through many years spent in the kitchen cooking breakfasts and brunches. Perhaps this is not as impressive as the last two, but sometimes all you need is a perfectly fried, poached or scrambled egg that heals the soul. You're lucky if you have tried it.

Impressiveness:
9/10
